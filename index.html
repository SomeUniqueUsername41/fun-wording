Wording to use more often

  • I'm not making this up (used to assure the listener that something is absolutely true, beyond a hint of doubt)

  • The show must go on!
  • ...or my name isn't "(insert name)"
  • Serves you right!

  • It can't be!
  • (Person, place, thing, or idea) is the last thing that comes to mind when I think of (something completely different or unrelated)
  • It's not like... (insert a concept or scenario that's not true or is otherwise unlikely)
  • This is the last straw!
  • I just (insert action word) here.
  • If I told you once, I told you a million times...
  • (subject/topic) is/are a different story.

  • ...for all I care.
  • My thought(s)/sentiments/plan, etc. exactly.

  • It's quiet. Too quiet.
  • That's "(title/name you want yourself to be called)" to you!
  • For the last time, (something you've been trying to make clear, but the listener just won't listen)!
  • In other words...
  • That makes no difference.
  • (You) don't have to ask me twice!
  • Here's an idea, (your idea).
  • (Subject) is/are/am no (what the subject is being called).
  • I can explain!
  • It's not what you think.
  • It's not what it looks like.
  • I concur.
  • I don't (action) with/from/for (insult or negative thing you're calling someone)!
  • (Action, feeling, or concept) will get you nowhere.
  • This is the thanks I get (either before or after saying what you've done or what you've been doing)
  • They don't call me... for nothing!

  • What? No (something or someone you were expecting or hoping for from a collection, assortment, compliation, team, etc.)?

  • How can that be?
  • How can it be?

  • This again?
  • Howsabout...?
  • What say we...?
  • You think (statement that's unlikely or not true)?
